Comparison

Lunr + Elasticlunr

  • Inverted indices
  • Indexing is O(n)O(n)O(n)
  • Searching is O(1)O(1)O(1)
  • Powerful fuzzy matching
  • Larger footprint

MiniSearch

  • Radix tree
  • Small footprint
  • Indexing is O(n)O(n)O(n)
  • Searching is O(1)O(1)O(1)
  • Limited partial (prefix) and fuzzy matching

Bloom Search

  • Bloom filters
  • Smallest footprint
  • Indexing and searching are O(k×n)O(k \times n)O(k×n)
  • Very limited fuzzy matching
  • False positive rate can be lowered but never eliminated
